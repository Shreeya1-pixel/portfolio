Literary Journey

From childhood bookworm to published author

Early Years

Time and again my parents used to remind me in my formative years that books are our real treasure and best friends. I became a member of a prestigious book club at our school library and attended a summer workshop at the British Council in Delhi, playing an important character in 'Charlie and the Chocolate Factory'.

Growing Connection

As I grew up, I felt most connected to the reading community. The bookshelf at home was my comfort and go-to place. We discuss 'the world of Jane Austen and Emily Bront√´', creating an intense bond amongst all readers, writers and book club members.

First Publication

My first poem got published in a book about women empowerment. A young reader contacted me, sharing how deeply connected she felt to my poem and how it encouraged her to raise her voice against injustice. This inspired me to continue spreading my literary words.

Interdisciplinary Excellence

As the founder of Network company and a published author with a chapter in "Musings of Life," I believe in the power of interdisciplinary thinking. My achievements in volleyball as a state-level player and team captain of National Volleyball Team India have taught me leadership, while chess has sharpened my strategic thinking.
